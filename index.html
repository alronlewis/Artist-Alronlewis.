About Me

Hi, I’m Alron Lewis, a self-taught traditional artist since 2020. I specialize in graphite medium and enjoy creating both realistic and anime-style art. With years of dedication, I focus on fine details and storytelling through every artwork. For me, art is not just a hobby — it’s a way of life.
